Account deletion

Delete Your Chronicler Account Data

Chronicler is published by Ferhat Acar. You can delete your account data in the app or request deletion by email.

Request deletion by email

Send an email to 3amfreak@gmail.com with the subject Chronicler account deletion request.

Include the Google sign-in email address you used with Chronicler so the correct account data can be identified.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Chronicler.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Account and data.
  4. Choose Delete account data.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

What Will Be Deleted

Chronicler will delete app account data associated with your Chronicler account, including journal entries, saved stories, answers, and app profile data stored for the app.

What May Be Retained

Some limited records may be retained when necessary for security, fraud prevention, purchase records, legal obligations, or service operation. Google Play purchase history is managed by Google Play.

Questions

For any deletion or privacy question, contact Ferhat Acar at 3amfreak@gmail.com.
