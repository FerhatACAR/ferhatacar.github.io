Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Chronicler

Effective date: June 1, 2026

Chronicler is a journaling app published by Ferhat Acar. This Privacy Policy explains how Chronicler handles information when you use the app.

Information Chronicler Handles

Chronicler may process the following information to provide the app:

  • Google sign-in information used with Firebase Authentication, such as your account identifier and email address.
  • Journal answers, generated story drafts, saved stories, app profile settings, and subscription status stored in Firebase services.
  • Purchase and subscription information needed to validate Premium access through Google Play.
  • Rewarded ad and consent information used to show ads and manage ad preferences.
  • Crash, diagnostics, analytics, app configuration, and usage events used to improve reliability and understand app performance.
  • Reminder preferences stored on your device for local notifications.

How Information Is Used

Chronicler uses information to:

  • Sign you in and keep your journal associated with your account.
  • Save, edit, export, and delete your journal entries and stories.
  • Generate editable story drafts from your daily answers.
  • Validate subscriptions and purchases.
  • Show rewarded ads when you choose to use them.
  • Send local reminder notifications if you enable reminders.
  • Monitor crashes, app health, and feature performance.

Third-Party Services

Chronicler uses service providers that may process data according to their own policies:

  • Google Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Functions, Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config, and App Check.
  • Google Sign-In for account access.
  • Google Mobile Ads / AdMob for rewarded ads and ad consent flows.
  • Google Play Billing for subscriptions and in-app purchases.
  • Google Gemini or related backend AI services for story generation through Chronicler's backend.

Journal Content and AI Drafts

Chronicler turns your daily answers into editable first-person journal drafts. You should review and edit generated drafts before saving or sharing them. Avoid entering information that you do not want processed by the app or its service providers.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your Chronicler account data inside the app from Settings > Account and data > Delete account data. You can also request deletion by email from the Delete Account page.

Some limited records may be retained when necessary for security, fraud prevention, purchase records, legal obligations, or service operation.

Children

Chronicler is not intended for children. Do not use Chronicler if you are not old enough to use online services in your country or region.
